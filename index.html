Bangladesh Launches New Satellite

Bangladesh to Launch Second Satellite

Date: 05/01/2025

Time: 10.25 PM

The Bangladeshi government plans to launch a second satellite, Bangabandhu Satellite-2, within the next four years. The satellite will be used for Earth observation and disaster management. Bangladesh has signed a memorandum of understanding with Russia's Glavkosmos for the launch of the satellite.

Bangabandhu Satellite-1 Provides Connectivity to 39,000 Ships

Date: 05/01/2025

Time: 10.25 PM

Bangabandhu Satellite-1 is providing connectivity to around 39,000 ships in the Bay of Bengal and inland ports of Bangladesh. The satellite has 40 transponders, 20 of which are designated for local use and the other 20 can be used outside the country.
